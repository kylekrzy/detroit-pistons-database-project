Question 2: Show all players ordered by salary from highest to lowest.

Question 3: Show all staff members in the Coaching Staff department.

Question 5: Show all players who have at least one performance record.

Question 6: Show all players who do not have any performance records yet.

Question 7: Show all tickets that are still available or reserved.

Question 8: Show all merchandise items that have at least one sale recorded.

Question 9: Show all finance records with status "Pending".
